About the Team

Royal Challengers Bengaluru (RCB), formerly Royal Challengers Bangalore, is a franchise cricket team based in Bengaluru, Karnataka. The team has a massive and passionate fanbase and plays its home matches at the M. Chinnaswamy Stadium. After many years of coming close, the team finally achieved its first-ever IPL title victory in the 2025 season by defeating Punjab Kings in the final. The team's success is a testament to its star-studded lineup and the unwavering support of its fans.
